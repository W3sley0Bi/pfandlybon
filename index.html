Three simple steps

How Pfandly works

1

Return your bottles

Take your empty bottles to any supermarket return machine. Grab the printed Pfandbon the machine hands you — that's your deposit receipt.

2

Scan the paper bon

Tap + in Pfandly and point your camera at the receipt. The AI scanner reads the value and barcode automatically. Your bon is saved — paper safely binned.

3

Redeem at checkout

At any self-checkout that accepts Pfandbons, open Pfandly and show the barcode. The cashier or machine scans it and the value comes off your total.
